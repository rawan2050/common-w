What is an Account Tackeover?

Account takeover occurs when cybercriminals gain unauthorized access to financial accounts using stolen credentials.

Common Cyber Threats

Phishing Emails

Fake emails designed to steal usernames, passwords, and financial information.

Social Engineering

Attackers impersonate trusted organizations to obtain sensitive information.

Data Breaches

Stolen credentials from breached websites are often reused in attacks.

8 Essential Security Tips

🔒 Use Strong Passwords
🔑 Enable Multi-Factor Authentication
📧 Avoid Clicking Suspicious Links
💻 Keep Devices Updated
📱 Use Trusted Devices Only
🌐 Access Secure HTTPS Websites
📶 Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Financial Transactions
📊 Monitor Account Activity Regularly

What Should You Do?

What To Do If You Suspect an Attack?

    Change Passwords

    Immediately update passwords for affected accounts.

    Enable MFA

    Activate multi-factor authentication for extra protection.

    Review Activity

    Check transactions and account activity for suspicious actions.

    Contact Institution

    Notify your bank or financial provider immediately.

    Report Incident

    Inform your IT Security team about the suspicious activity.
